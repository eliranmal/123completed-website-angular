Gigaspaces Technologies Partnership

123Completed, a leader in providing services to high-end software projects, has entered a partnership with GigaSpaces, a leading provider of application virtualization platforms and cloud enablement solutions. 123Completed will be a solution implementer of GigaSpaces technologies, and will be the principal GigaSpaces training provider worldwide. Gershon Diner, GigaSpaces VP Customer Services & Support, says: "We are glad to have 123Completed as a partner, and look forward to benefitting from 123Completed expertise and great service in various fields such as product development, architecture, customer consultancy, and training."
