Methodology

At 1 2 3 Completed, we use a proven software development method to provide our customers with the best practices of development & database technologies. Our software development methodology is based on best of breed practices in the industry and on years of hands on experience. As part of our methodology, we identify recurring design patterns and utilize them to reduce development efforts. By doing so, we allow our customers not only to enable new functionality in their applications, but also to improve performance, stability and usability. We use UML, Requirements Analysis, Domain Modeling, Object–Oriented Analysis, Architectural Design, Object–Oriented Design, Structured Implementation and Project Management.
